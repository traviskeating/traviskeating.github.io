Biography

Why hello there! My name is Travis "T3" Keating. If you're curious about how and/or why I put 'T3' in between my first and last names, While this is a valid question I feel little need in explaining it beyond the fact that it has to do with basketball and tractor trailers. Where are my manners? You've clicked all this way to come here and all I've done is blather on about nothing. *AHEM* Now where was I? Oh yes, I am a Software Development major at Georgia Gwinnett College and I am currently in my 9th semester, that is to say, the start of my fifth year. "But Travis, why would you stay in college for 5 years when you are seeking a major that only takes 4 years to complete?" Well my dear reader that is a very astute observation followed by a question that my extended family often asks me at our occasional family functions. You see dear reader, I was not always a disciple of data, a connoisseur of code, a practitioner of Python, a sophisticate of software. There was a darker time in my life when I was a biology major, and there was an even darker time before that when I was a psychology major. It was not until I discovered the Unity Game Engine that I found my true calling as a video game developer. As it stands now I am but a somewhat humble journeyman in that great proffesion that is called Software Development.
