My Leisure activities

Or how I spend my free time

On the off chance that you did not notice or chose not to notice my small introduction to my leisurely activities on the front page (or perhaps you simply wish to read them again) I shall now tell you of how I spend my free time.

Gaming

A large portion of my time is spent gaming. I enjoy not only those games published in a digital medium, but also those published inthe analog medium, with my passion being more for the latter than for the former (though such a comparison is like asking someone if they prefer an amazing steak over a slightly more amazing steak).

Now when I speak of analog games, I of course am referring to board games, crad games, war minature games, and of course table top RPGs (TRPGs if you will). I find the face to face nature of this medium to be what gives these games their charm (the additional benefit of not having to listen to prepubescent boys yelling at me over their microphones and the lack of glitches make the games all the more enjoyable). Of the afore mentioned game genres I have a great fondness of TRPGs, particularly Dungeons & Dragons. I have played TRPGs since I was but a wee lad of only 10 years. However I don't just play D&D (and other TRPG systems such as FATE, 13th Age, Corvus Bellie Infinity, Apocalypse World, etc.) I have also moderated (that is to say I created and maintained) numerous campaigns, and as a result I have created numerous worlds for my players to explore. If you should wish to learn more about my most recent campaign world I would direct you to my campaign wiki page

Painting

I suppose I can't really count painting as a separate hobby from gaming seing as how I paint small plastic figurines called 'miniature' that are used in TRPGs to represent characters on the battlefield. The art of miniatures painting recquires a meticulous eye for detail and a steady hand in order to be succesful but I consider the pay off to be more than enough to compensate the countless hours and money I've spent on paint, miniatures, brushes, and other implements of painting.
